Our Firm

Brookes is a prominent full service Consulting, Accounting and Auditing firm. We are a progressive, forward-looking firm on the forefront of the accounting profession.

At Brookes, a creative approach is an integral part of every engagement. Intelligence and imagination combined with sound professional judgment enables the development and evaluation of new concepts while remaining sensitive to the need for practical solutions. This approach allows for solutions to financial problems that are innovative and effective.

Client service forms the foundation of our business philosophy. We are committed to delivering cost-effective, professional and reliable services that give our clients a great value in many ways.

Our Values

  • Quality: What we do, we do well
  • Integrity: Be real
  • Commitment: Do commit to our clients
  • Accountability: Always in our clients’ support

Our Standards

Our client service standards are followed throughout the firm to ensure quality service to all our clients

  • We identify, on each engagement, and analyze our clients’ needs and meet their expectations.
  • We develop client service objectives that will enable us to fulfill our professional responsibilities, satisfy our client’s needs, and exceed their expectations.
  • We provide our clients with insight analysis for their business issues and practical solutions
  • We communicate effectively with our clients, both internal and external, to enhance client perceptions of the value and quality of our service.
  • Help management with insights on their business and meaningful suggestions for improvement.
  • Create strategic relationship with our clients and update them from time to time on the latest changes in laws / standards affecting their business.
  • Meeting the deadline and submission dates with our clients is one of our priorities.
  • Respect our clients and consider the difficult situation while submitting our engagement fees to ensure that we receive fees that reflect the value of services provided and responsibilities assumed.
